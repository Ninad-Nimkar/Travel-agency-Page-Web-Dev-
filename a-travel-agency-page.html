Taj Mahal

Explore the timeless beauty of the Taj Mahal in Agra, India—one of the world’s most iconic monuments. Travel options include guided tours from Delhi, luxury train journeys like the Palace on Wheels, or self-planned trips via road or rail. Sunrise and sunset visits offer the most breathtaking views. Don’t miss nearby attractions like Agra Fort and Fatehpur Sikri for a complete historical experience.
